Weight Loss Stall Protocol

If weight loss stalls for 2 consecutive weeks (less than 0.5 lb/week), implement in this order:

  1. Step 1: Calorie Adjustment

    Reduce daily calories by 100-150 (from carbs), maintaining protein.

  2. Step 2: Macronutrient Redistribution

    Increase protein to 1.8-2.0g/kg, reduce carbs by 20-30g.

  3. Step 3: Meal Timing Adjustment

    Ensure protein is distributed evenly (25-30g per meal). Add protein snack 30 min before workouts.

  4. Step 4: Activity Adjustment

    Increase daily steps by 2,000-3,000. Add one 20-min moderate cardio session.

  5. Step 5: Reassessment

    If no progress after 2 weeks, reduce calories by another 100. Consider healthcare check-in.

Additional Success Resources

Sleep Optimization

  • Consistent bedtime: Same time daily.
  • Wind-down routine: 30 min of relaxing activities.
  • Screen reduction: No screens 1 hour before bed.
  • Cool environment: 60-67F (15-19C).

Menstrual Cycle

  • Follicular (Wk 1-2): Higher energy, ideal for intense training.
  • Ovulation (Mid): Peak performance, good for PR attempts.
  • Luteal (Wk 3-4): Lower energy, focus on consistency.

Managing Disordered Eating History

  • Regular meal timing: Set alarms if needed.
  • Balanced approach: Avoid "good/bad" food labels.
  • Mindful eating: Pay attention to hunger/fullness cues.
  • Body function focus: Appreciate what your body can do.

Eating Out Strategy

  • Check menu online beforehand.
  • Decide on options before arriving.
  • Request dressings on side, cooking method changes.
  • Consider sharing or taking half home.

  • Given your history of disordered eating, be mindful of any obsessive thoughts about food or exercise.

Consistency Over Perfection

  • If you miss a workout or have an off-plan meal, don't worryjust get back on track.
